Journey of my heart
The Law of Attraction believes your thoughts create your reality. Focus on what you want, not what you lack. Visualize it, feel the positive emotions, and believe it is already yours. This alignment attracts your desires into your life.
Taisha Book teaches that true wisdom is within. See beyond illusions of separation. Its core principle is love and unity. By embracing the present moment, you remember your true self. You are already complete.
A Course in Miracles teaches forgiveness as the path to inner peace. It emphasizes that love is our only reality and that we can choose to see through the illusions of fear and guilt, guided by the Holy Spirit.
I Ching uses 64 hexagrams to interpret life’s dynamic patterns. By consulting it, one seeks harmony with the universe’s natural flow, balancing yin and yang to guide decisions and understand change.
